AutoCAD Panel — How It Works

The SANGAM panel lives inside AutoCAD as a dockable WPF panel. No browser needed.

1

Open AutoCAD

Panel loads automatically, shows your assignments. Your project and assigned supports appear instantly based on your login credentials.

2

Click a Support

DWG downloaded from server and opened in AutoCAD. The drawing file is fetched from the SANGAM server and opened directly in the active viewport.

3

Click Submit

File pushed to server, status updated, timestamp recorded. The modified DWG is uploaded back, revision tracked, and the support moves to review.

No browser. No download. No upload. Everything inside AutoCAD.
